Our Programs

Eight sports. All skill levels. Open to every child in Pike County. Find the program that's right for your young athlete.

All Programs & Sports

Registration is open for all spring and fall programs. Scholarship assistance is available — no child is turned away for inability to pay. Contact us to learn more.

Basketball

Ages 8–18

Our flagship program features skill-building clinics, recreational leagues, and competitive travel teams for grades 3 through 12. Coaches are certified youth instructors focused on fundamentals, teamwork, and basketball IQ.

Fall & Winter Seasons Recreational + Competitive Certified Coaches
60+ athletes enrolled each season

Soccer

Ages 5–16

Spring and fall soccer leagues for all skill levels. From first-time players learning to dribble to competitive travel athletes, our certified youth soccer coaches meet each child where they are.

Spring & Fall Seasons All Skill Levels Co-Ed Available
50+ athletes enrolled each season

Track & Field

Ages 7–18

Speed, strength, and mental discipline. Our track and field program develops well-rounded athletes across sprints, distance, jumps, and throws. Athletes compete at regional youth meets throughout the season.

Spring Season Regional Competition Multi-Event
30+ athletes compete annually
Register
⚾

Baseball & Softball

Ages 4–18

From tee-ball to travel-level play, our baseball and softball programs give young athletes a love for America's pastime. Separate boys and girls leagues are available, as well as co-ed recreational divisions.

Spring Season Tee-Ball to Travel Boys & Girls Leagues
45+ athletes per season
Register
🏈

Flag Football

Ages 6–12

A safe, high-energy introduction to football fundamentals — no contact, all competition. Perfect for younger athletes who want to learn routes, snaps, and strategy in a fun, low-pressure environment.

Fall Season Non-Contact Beginner Friendly
40+ athletes per fall season
Register
🏐

Volleyball

Ages 8–16

Indoor and outdoor volleyball leagues teach communication, agility, and team cohesion. Our program runs seasonal leagues plus summer open-gym sessions for athletes who want extra court time.

Spring & Summer Indoor & Outdoor Open Gym Sessions
25+ athletes per season
