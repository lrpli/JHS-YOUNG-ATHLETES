The People Behind the Programs

Meet Our Team

Dedicated coaches, passionate volunteers, and committed leaders — the people of JHS Young Athletes show up every day for the kids of Pike County.

Leadership

Board & Staff

MH

Marcus Henderson

Executive Director

A lifelong Pike County resident and former high school coach, Marcus has led JHS Young Athletes for over a decade, growing it from a small recreational league into a cornerstone of the Eolia community.

LT

Linda Torres

Board President

Linda brings 20 years of nonprofit leadership experience to JHS Young Athletes. Her focus on financial sustainability and community partnerships has been instrumental in the organization's growth.

DJ

DeShawn Johnson

Athletic Director

Former collegiate athlete and certified youth sports instructor, DeShawn oversees all programming, coach development, and athletic standards across every sport we offer.

SR

Sandra Reeves

Community Outreach

Sandra connects JHS Young Athletes with local schools, churches, and businesses to ensure our programs reach every corner of Pike County — especially families with the greatest need.

TW

Tom Wicker

Treasurer

Tom handles the financial operations of JHS Young Athletes with precision and transparency, ensuring every dollar donated goes directly to supporting our young athletes.

AM

Angela Martin

Volunteer Coordinator

Angela recruits, trains, and manages the incredible network of 30+ volunteers who make our programs possible. Her organizational energy keeps everything running smoothly.
